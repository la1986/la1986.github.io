Magic Hash Brownies

Magic Hash Brownies are one of the tastiest treats the world has ever known. Few have tried them and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 200g cocoa powder
  • 1 large bar of chocolate
  • 1 large bag of cali kush

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, cocoa powder, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Add in the special ingredient (Cali Kush)
  3. Spread into a non-stick dish
  4. Sprinkle chocolate all over
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  6. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  7. Eat and enjoy the ride!
